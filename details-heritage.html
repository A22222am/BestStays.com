Heritage Shophouse

Jonker Walk, Malacca

Description

Stay in a beautifully restored traditional Peranakan house situated right in the heart of the historical district. You will be instantly captivated by the authentic interior courtyard that wonderfully floods the space with natural light. The vibrant and famous Jonker Walk night market is just a short, very pleasant stroll away. Antique wooden furnishings seamlessly blend with modern comforts to create a highly unique atmosphere. It provides an immersive cultural experience that you will treasure long after your trip ends.

Facilities & Amenities

  • 2 Bedrooms, 2 Baths
  • Traditional Courtyard
  • Walking Distance to Night Market
  • Vintage Decor & Architecture

Location

Address: Jalan Hang Jebat (Jonker Street), 75200 Melaka
